The Trunk in the Attic

Decades after the war, a small wooden trunk was found in the attic of the Whitmore family home. Inside were personal belongings of Private Thomas Whitmore — preserved memories of a life interrupted by war.

Item 1: Field Diary

The diary contains rough pencil sketches of the trenches, short notes, and dates. One entry reads: “January 3rd — rain again. Jam for dinner. Fritz still quiet.”

Item 2: Family Photograph

This photo was folded in his coat pocket. On the back was written: “Home is never far from the heart.”

Item 3: Letter Never Sent

“Tell Sarah not to worry. I'll be home before harvest. I promise.”
